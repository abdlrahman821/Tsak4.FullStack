About Us

Our Story

TechSolutions was founded in 2010 with a mission to help businesses establish a strong online presence. What started as a small team of two developers has now grown into a full-service digital agency with over 50 employees.

We believe in the power of technology to transform businesses and create meaningful connections with customers. Our team is passionate about creating digital solutions that not only look great but also deliver measurable results.

Over the years, we've worked with businesses of all sizes, from startups to Fortune 500 companies, across various industries including healthcare, education, retail, and technology.
